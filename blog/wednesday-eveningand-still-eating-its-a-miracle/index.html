Wednesday evening…and still eating! It’s a miracle!

July 1, 2009 Cara 1 Comment

Good evening!  Well like the title says it’s Wednesday evening now…by now I’m usually curled up in a ball sleeping, not talking (seriously – me not talking!), not eating and being sick.  This time I’ve been chatting (yes back to being me!) and eating like a horse (not a gift horse or a hungry horse or any of those silly sayings!) and not been or felt sick at all…TOUCH WOOD… I really don’t want to curse this as after another sleepless night with 4 different chemos going in it could be a very different story on the sickness front tomorrow…

…I’m really glad I stayed on this ward today and didn’t go into my own room cos the day’s gone really quickly with other people around.  & I’ve been keeping myself busy chatting online and emailing and I’ve taken to colouring now!  I find it really therapeutic and addictive so it’s working for me at the moment & anything that works is worth a try!  I’ve reverted to being a child (some of you may think that hasn’t been much of a transformation!) with my new colouring books and pencils and taking my bear everywhere!  I will post a photo of my bear on here soon as he started off naked as bears often are!  He’s now got a Team Scamp t-shirt on (it’s an early baby one!  Anna made it for him bless her!  Thanks scampi!!)  Anna will have so much to read and catch up on when she gets back as she’s away for 2 weeks at the moment and I’m missing her lots but texting lots!  Anyway so today was another good day and has gone really quickly.  My mum’s been with me during the day as always which is always lovely company.  Chris came this evening after working in Wales bless him and Gemma W and Chris O came to see me too which was lovely as I haven’t seen them for ages.  I haven’t seen lots of people for ages cos I haven’t been up to it for so long but I’m feeling better now than I have for a long time…let’s hope I’m not jinxing it by going on about it!

Thank you for all your comments those of you who wrote them and everyone who’s let me know you’re reading…sounds like there’s a fair few addicts out there!  What will you do when I’m better?!  I’m not looking forward to tonight as it’s all the drug changes again all through the night so I won’t get much sleep again and I’m just not feeling tired for the first time in weeks if not months, I actually feel quite hyper!  It must be all the food….my doctor told me today to eat as much junk as I can!!  OK!!  It’s jaffa cakes I’m addicted to at the moment!  & pasta!  Funny how things change all the time…

Anyway night night I hope I’ll be back tomorrow…if not you’ll know I’ve deteriorated overnight…here’s hoping NOT!

Love and hugs

Scamp xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
