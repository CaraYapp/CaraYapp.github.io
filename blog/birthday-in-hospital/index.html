Birthday in hospital :-(

August 1, 2009 Cara

Well most of you will probably know by now that I ended up back in hospital on Thursday.  I was coming in to have my blood count checked as a precaution anyway as that’s usually the time things start to go wrong and we needed to be safe this time after ending up in intensive care last time.  Then I woke up on Thursday with a sore throat and my mouth had got a lot worse so when we told the Professor this he said to admit me immediately cos we can’t take any chances this time.  Then it turned out from checking my blood count that I definitely needed to be here anyway!  I was (still am!) neutropenic and also anaemic again.  I needed a 3 unit blood transfusion this time and platelets and to start antibiotics and fluids again.  So…here I am again!  I haven’t had much sleep since I’ve got here as it’s a constant flow of nurses needing to do this that and the other!  It’s now my birthday and I’ve been awake since 5 am…it’s so annoying being woken that early when you’ve got a day of sitting in your room ahead of you!  Oh well, I’m obviously disappointed to be here on my birthday but I did expect it and I’m sure it’ll be a lovely day and my visitors will make it special for me.  & then I’m hoping to get home for a few days again next week before going back for chemo so hopefully I can have a 2nd birthday!  Anna and I did decide though that as this year of my life has been stolen from me that I shouldn’t have to age a year!  So does that mean I’m not 27 today, I’m only 26?!  Or maybe I could go back to 21?!  I really don’t like the number 27, it’s bugging me and I don’t want to change my age at the top of this web page!  I’m sure that can wait until tomorrow at least!  I’m still 26 until 7pm tonight when I was born!

Right well I’m being bugged by nurses again so I’m off!  Chris should be here soon…he’s coming in early today to see the birthday girl!(that’s me!)

Lots of love xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
