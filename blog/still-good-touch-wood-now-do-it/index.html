Still good! Touch WOOD!! NOW!! DO IT!!

July 2, 2009 Cara 2 Comments

Hi all,

Really not going to write much as haven’t had much sleep and although I don’t feel too tired, tonight I shall be woken at midnight, 1.30, 5.30. 7, 8.30 or there abouts so I won’t get too much sleep so I’d better start trying soon!

I’ve had another ‘good’  day, no tears, still managing to eat and no sickness…touch wood!!  I’m so scared of cursing it.  We’re now addicted to playing Uno, does anyone know it?  We love it, Chris is very competitive and likes to win!  Goose (my mum) is getting that way too!

Today I’ve spent some lovely time with Goose out on the roof garden (in the shade of course!) Then Chris arrived earlier as managed to get away from work in Wales a bit earlier which was lovely…he brought in a lovely picture of us in a frame from home from when we were on holiday in Portugal last summer so that made me smile too and the other ladies here and the nurses have just been admiring it!!  & my long blonde hair in it that I was probably stressing about looking horrible at the time looks perfectly LOVELY to me now!!

Anyway then my dad came for a bit too and tonight Mr and Mrs Bear AKA Ollie and Laura have been to see me.  They brought subways but I’m not really allowed them but so I missed out but at least I know Chris has been fed tonight…I do worry!  Anyway so we’ve been sitting out on the roof garden too which has been lovely…a really nice warm but cool summer’s evening – we were saying we needed to set up a few disposable barbies (BBQs not barbie dolls!) out there!  Anyway it was really really lovely to see them as it’s been a long time.  Apart from them both looking very brown as they’ve just got back from holiday…especially Laura – I warned her I’d scratch her eyes out if she was too brown but she didn’t listen!  I managed to resist as it was too lovely to see her to start beating her up!!

Well I’m signing off now…until tomorrow (today for probably most of you reading this!)  I spend alot of the day online at the moment so if anyone’s bored at work (or at home) come onto facebook to chat or email me!! & keep the comments coming, I love reading them!!

Big hugs and sloppy kisses!!

Scamp xxx
