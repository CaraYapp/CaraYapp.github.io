Bald as brass!

November 19, 2009 Cara 1 Comment

I can’t believe I forgot to write in my post that yesterday I went round Sainsbury’s bald!  Breaking news! It’s the first time I’ve done it in public and it was actually fine!  I’d been round a few shops in Fleet and got really really hot so when we got back to the car I took my wig off and slung it on the dashboard (see below!)  So we went to Sainsbury’s and I just thought, why am I going to put my wig back on?  It’s certainly not for my own comfort, it’s basically to make other people feel comfortable so I blend in and don’t stand out as the girl with the “I’ve got cancer” beacon on my head!  (I don’t actually have one of those you understand, THAT would be weird!)  So I just did it.  & it was fine.  I could feel people looking and could read their minds and at times I felt like shouting out “Yes I’ve got cancer!” to answer their thoughts!  But I  managed to get round the shop without shouting at anyone and I didn’t even feel that stared at to be honest, I didn’t pay any attention, just got on with my shopping.  & who cares anyway?!

I did fall in love with these Innocent smoothies with little tiny knitted bobble hats on though.  I had to buy them, they were looking at me!  & now they match me with my wooly hat!  I wish they fitted me!
