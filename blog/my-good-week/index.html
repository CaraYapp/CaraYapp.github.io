My ‘good’ week…

November 5, 2009 Cara

This week has been nice and busy and full of things I love to do and people I love to see!  The weekend was very wedding orientated…no, not mine!  On Saturday I went wedding dress shopping with Laura again…this time Gemma W (her other bridesmaid) and I went with her to Basingstoke…but there was an accident on the M3 and it took us over 2 hours to get from Fleet to Basingstoke!  The other side of the motorway was totally closed and our side was at a complete standstill so everyone was turning their engines off and getting out of their cars.  I chose not to though, didn’t fancy hobbling around on my crutches just for the sake of it…and didn’t want my wig blowing off on the M3, even if it was a temporary car park at the time!  Instead, us girls passed the time by doing what we do best…chatting…and looking at firemen!  Luck was on our side as there was a fire engine right next to us and we found out that if we looked at it long enough, firemen appeared out of it!  & they were not disappointing!  Well, one of them wasn’t anyway!  I’ll leave the rest to your imaginations!  Anyway we eventually got there and Laura tried on some beautiful dresses…like a true princess!  I’ll say no more as they’re top secret…the quest for ‘the one’ continues though!  Saturday night was spent with Simon Cowell in the world of X Factor…Chris was away for the weekend – he went to stay with his brother in Manchester, although after a night out on the beers with his bro and bro in law, his brother ‘wandered off’ which is what he does best after a few drinks, so predictably they didn’t get home together and Chris had to go home with Darren his bro in law instead, waking up his poor sister Emma!  So I had a very peaceful night’s sleep!  On Sunday I went with Ollie & Laura (& Gemma) to see the venue for their wedding (Ollie & Laura’s, not Ollie, Laura & Gemma’s!)…it’s absolutely lovely and really nice to picture now where the big day will be.  Although I’m now petrified because we’ve got to walk down a very grand staircase into the room where they’ll get married…I’ll probably trip and create a domino effect knocking Gemma and Laura down the stairs!  & then if Laura’s trying to steal my wig, we’ll have a fight!  It’ll be a memorable day anyway!

Then the highlights of this week have been:

Lunch & the afternoon spent with my sister and the scamps (my nieces!)

A mammoth shopping trip to Primark…it’s been far too long so I went a bit crazy!  No guilt involved though as everything’s so bargainous!  Love that place!

A bit more shopping…oops!

A dinner out & a good chat with Gemma & Lou

A visit to my Nana, also with Goose, my aunt Corinne, my sister and the scamps again!

A morning making cupcakes and making lots of mess and licking lots of spoons! (I’ll post photos soon!)

An afternoon with Gillie spent eating the cupcakes and chatting away!…and giving the grand tour of our lovely house!

…tonight we’re having fajitas, mmmm my favourite and watching a film of my choice!  No doubt Chris will be asleep soon anyway as he’s only just got back from working in Wales.

Tomorrow is back to the world of hospitals for an appointment in clinic with Prof.   Hopefully there’ll be no surprises and no tears…but I’ll take my eyelash glue just in case!  Chemo is on Monday and is now looming…but I’ve got lots to look forward to first….friends round tomorrow night for some food and games (Wii time!) and then we’re going away with Ollie & Laura for the weekend to stay with Ally & Emma, so looking forward to that.

I’ve got so much in my head I want to write on here, some good, some not so good…but I’ll split into day by day so I don’t bombard you all at once.  Thank  you for my comments, and thanks to the new commenters, I love finding out who’s been reading!

Love you all xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
