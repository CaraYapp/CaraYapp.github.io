Day 2 of Chemo…or not so much chemo in fact! Trust me to have problems!

April 29, 2009 Cara 1 Comment

Well I’ve done my first night…sound like I’m in prison!  It’s actually very nice here and I’m being taken care of very well.  Having all my stuff here is helping ALOT!  Quite nice at the moment in a very strange way, just listening to music, reading, watching movies, spending lots of money on ebay, oh DEAR!  Chris will be bombarded with parcels arriving at home, it’ll be like Christmas!!

Well last night turned out to be very complicated …yes even more complicated than I explained that it was planned to be!  Basically I finished my first 4 hour red drug (no pink pee, sorry to disappoint people!) then they flushed the drip through which was all fine but then within minutes or even seconds of drug number 2 going in, I reacted not too well!  Temperature shot up instantly and I was on fire, my eyes started to swell up, my breathing was really tight and went really dizzy and bit nauseous.  Sure you don’t want these details but oh well!  Tough!  I’m not leaving anything out, for this to lead to an international bestseller, it has to have gory details!  Although not too many of them…yet!

Anyway so they had to stop it for obvious reasons and within minutes I was fine again.  I must be allergic or something to something in that drug….waiting for them to decide and let me know what the next step is.  Annoying as I’ll end up being like at least 12 hours behind my chemo schedule so I’ll probs be having to stay in til at least Sunday to get it all into me…that’s if my body can cope!

Anyway, it’s not all doom and gloom, I had breakfast in bed and just been choosing my lunch and dinner from a massive menu!  Food’s actually pretty good – I’m stocking my belly up now (not that I need to!) just in case I’m sick soon and won’t be able to eat.  Plus we all know food can cure anything!!

That’s all for now folks…Chris Moyles is keeping me sane at the moment on the radio!  Think I’ll get on twitter and try to be his friend!!

 

They’ve just shown me there’s a bath!  How exciting!  I’m off to bathe and be clean and make the most of washing my hair while I still  can!

Adios amigos xxx
