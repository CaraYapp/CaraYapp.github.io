Goodbye 2009…at last!

December 31, 2009 Cara

I’ll be glad to see the end of 2009 and having been looking forward to New Year for that reason only…(I’m not a fan of New Year’s Eve generally!)  But now that it’s here it’s dawned on me that just because at midnight it becomes a new year, it doesn’t mean everything’s going to miraculously get better overnight.  Unfortunately.  Anyway I won’t be all doom and gloom…here’s hoping for a better 2010 on the whole.  I won’t say it can’t get any worse because it could and I’m not going to tempt fate.  I’m just feeling a bit low and fed up.  The peaceful, relaxing Christmas we were hoping for unfortunately did not happen.  I won’t go into too much detail on here but my dad ended up in hospital and got out on Christmas Eve but is still not well and is going for more tests next week.  As my dad was discharged, my Nana went into hospital and sadly had to spend Christmas there and is still there.  It seems the year wants to end on a low note for my family…a rubbish end to a rubbish year.  My sister even had someone skid into the wall in front of her house and flatten it & crash into their 2 cars on the driveway!  Thank goodness noone was hurt, but when does that ever happen?!  It’s just one thing after another at the moment.  I’m not feeling all that great and am having trouble with my leg.  A trip to Oxford for an appointment yesterday led to more scans and just brought it all back to me that I’m still a cancer patient and it’s not over til it’s over….which feels like it’ll be never for me at the moment.

Anyway, I know I haven’t been on here for a long time but I was trying to have a ‘break’ from it all for Christmas times…I did have some lovely times with family, friends and with Chris but it just hasn’t turned out to be the break we all so desperately needed.  I’m back in for chemo on Monday so I’ll just try to enjoy my time before then and start 2010 with a smile on my face (even if it’s hiding so much hurt, pain & worry behind the smile!)

I hope everyone reading has had a lovely Christmas and I wish you all a happy and HEALTHY New Year xxx
