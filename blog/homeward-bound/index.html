Homeward bound!

June 3, 2009 Cara

Hey,

Well today I’ve been told I can go home!  YAY!  I’m just waiting for my drugs to come to take home so I can be discharged.  It’s no wonder I’m not rattling with all the pills I’m taking!  I’m really looking forward to getting home and being ‘normal’ for a few days before chemo number 3.  Plus I’ll be home to watch The Apprentice tonight, yay!  Priorities!  I just want to curl up in my own bed and sleep and sleep and sleep!!

I now have a wig that I LOVE!  & it’s free on the NHS.  Worth £200!  Amazing really!  They sent me 2 again cos I wasn’t sure.  When I went to the place I had to try the styles on in really dark colours and choose the colours separately so they could be ordered.  They’re both gorgeous but one of them’s a bit too glam and long and I look a bit like Paris Hilton or Sharpay from HSM!  Not quite so natural or practical.  The one I’m going to keep is a lovely colour, really natural and not so long but still long and shaped really nicely.  Laura, Gemma W, Lou, Katie and Sarah all saw them last night!  We had a bit of a trying on session!  They all think you wouldn’t know it was a wig if you didn’t know but I’m not sure if they’re just being kind!!  It is amazing though and I do love it.  I’ve just got to find the guts to wear it now though!  If I make it to Katie’s party for a bit on Saturday I might have to brave it!  It’s funny cos I’ve also got the blonde bob style one that I bought before so now I can choose to have long or short hair and be able to swap in an instant!  Very bizarre!

I’ve now lost a stone on the ‘chemo diet’!!  I wouldn’t recommend it though!  I don’t feel like I’ve lost that much or look like I have but I’m not surprised with how little I’ve eaten and how much I’ve been sick!  Alot of it I think it muscle wasting away too.  My legs seem alot smaller, I think just cos they’ve barely been used!  I feel like Ariel from The Little Mermaid when she gets legs and is all wobbly and can’t walk on them!  I wish I was a mermaid – I was thinking, if I didn’t have legs I wouldn’t be able to have a tumour in my leg…I doubt you can get tumours in a mermaid’s tail….hmmmmmm.  Anyway I’m either her or bambi on ice!  I’m going to attempt to walk to the car when I leave which will be a big achievement!  I haven’t walked further than the toilet for 2 or 3 weeks now!

Yesterday Goose (my mum!) came in and we went on a little adventure around Frimley Park Hospital looking for places to sit outside.  We got an ice cream (which I could only eat a third of…I’ve changed!!) and found a little courtyard to sit in.  It was SO nice to be outside as I’ve missed ALL this amazing weather.  Plus I got dressed for the first time!  It was nice to have a pretty dress on instead of pj’s….although it’s a good job I love pj’s cos I’ve lived in them for weeks!  Primark’s profits are up thanks to me and my pj shopping recently!

Well I better go and start packing up my room which is full of pinkness!  I’ve been looking for lovely hotels to go to as me and Chris might go away for a night or 2 on my next so called ‘good’ weekend…just somewhere quiet and peaceful and luxurious not too far away.  I think we just deserve to get away for a few days!

Well lots of love to you all, ciao for now!!
