Cara Yapp

Friday 1st May

May 2, 2009 Cara

OK, now I won’t write loads but as I had to do Thursday in hindsight, I don’t want to get really behind!  Yesterday (Friday) was better.  I had chemo changes all night but then in the morning after the last change I managed to get some more sleep which I really needed.  My mum and dad came back and Linda and Barry came too (Chris’s parents)  It was lovely to see them all but not sure how lovely it is for people to see me!!  I’ve been really swollen and puffy…attractive!  I’ve only gone and put on weight!  But no, before you think it, I’m not the only person ever to defy medical science and put weight ON during chemo!!  They say it’s normal and expected, it’s from all the fluid being pumped into me and also the steroids…so they keep giving me something to make me pee more!  I just love being made to pee MORE when have to wheel my stupid chemo trolley across to the toilet, write my bed number onto a cardboard pan and leave my pee to be collected to be measured and tested!  Yes everytime!  When I get home I won’t know what to do – I’ll be asking Chris to collect it!!  No. not REALLY!

Sickness…

I haven’t actually been sick yet but that’s probs cos I’m on SOOOOOO many anti-sickness drugs it’s ridiculous!!  Am feeling sick though right now, when I have the 4 different chemo drugs all in a row it makes me feel sick…& my tastebuds are changing which they also warned me about!  Seem to change throughout the day though, think depends what’s been drip, drip, dripping into me!  But yesterday OJ tasted like acid, a plain roll tasted like metal and pringles I’m sure were covered in iron filings!  In fact I can’t even type about food, my stomach’s turning…

So Friday night, Chris came fairly early so we had about an hour together alone which was lovely.  He brought me some cookies and cupcakes and they didn’t taste like metal!!  Phew!!  Then Anna arrived, followed by Ollie and Laura.  It was lovely to see all of them and I felt quite calm and relaxed.  We just chatted as normal really….well remember normal now isn’t what normal used to be!  Anna got me some lovely pressies (Thank you!!) and even made me a Little Miss Scamp Mr Men card and made TEAM SCAMP wrapping paper!  Pink of course…very cool!  Ollie had set up my pretty new laptop and installed everything I needed.  She’s just so shiny!  Not sure why I’m referring to it as a person – Laura that’s your fault!!

Right I’m going to pipe down now, don’t feel too good,  It’s Sat morning and I feel sick and weak.  The ward’s practically empty now as most peeps gone home.  I have to move to the long stay ward around lunchtime.  The nurse asked me when the removal van is coming!  My dad asked the same!  They know me too well!  I don’t have THAT much stuff!

Today Anna’s coming back this morning and Chris is coming around lunchtime and Robin my bro is coming sometime (when he’s up!!)  My parents might come too but think they deserve a rest really!  Robin asked me on the phone the other day about the ‘viewing’ times!!  Simple mistake – he of course meant visiting times but made me chuckle, sounds like a zoo!  I wish I WAS at the zoo…!xxx
