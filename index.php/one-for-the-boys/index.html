One for the boys!

November 4, 2009 Cara

My last few posts have been very girly and focused on hair (wigs!) and eyelashes (just bought some more today!)  So today here’s a pic that my male readers might prefer…my scar.  Boys like scars don’t they?!  Well not on girls, my legs are NOT attractive now!  Not that I liked them before but I didn’t know how lucky I was!  But I’m sure if I was a boy I’d be showing off my ‘war wound’?!  So here it is, it gives you an idea of the extent of the surgery…  I’ve just realised how dodgy that sounds – One for the boys!  Sorry if I got your hopes up, it’s nothing like THAT!! 

I’ve decided that telling people it’s a shark bite is a much more interesting story!  I was swimming in the ocean when Bruce from Finding Nemo came along and for a second he forgot that he’s a vegetarian and took a big bite but then he remembered and spat me out!  He felt really bad and was very sorry.  Bear (my bear, not Ollie!) scared him off and he was never to be seen again!

On a more serious note, I’m actually thinking about doing a course from home in creative writing…it was Goose’s idea and a very good one I think.  I’ve always wanted to be an author since I was about 3 I think!  I’ve always dreamed of writing children’s books but now I’d also love to write adult books (not as in ADULT books though!  Ha ha!)  but most of all at the moment I want to write my story of cancer as a book.  I think doing a course would give my brain a focus and would also be something productive to gain from my time.  I’m investigating it at the moment, there’s a few I’ve come across including How to Write a Bestseller and Children’s book writing…watch this space!

Right, I shall try to write more later…I’m busy this week trying to fit everything I want to do in and everyone I want to see…which is impossible.  Monday’s return to chemo is now looming and Friday’s taken up with yet another visit to Oxford to see Prof again.  The only trouble is, as I’m so busy, I’m wearing myself out and am feeling the strain!  Thanks again for all your comments and emails I’ve had too…if you’d rather email me than comment on here my email address is cara_yapp@yahoo.co.uk…

Bye for now xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
