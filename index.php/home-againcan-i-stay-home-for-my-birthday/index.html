Home again…can I stay home for my birthday?!

July 26, 2009 Cara

I’m home again…got home yesterday afternoon and I’m feeling ok but just totally wiped out from it all this time, am absolutely shattered and exhausted.  I’m just really hoping that I’ll manage to stay at home until Saturday as it’s my birthday!  I’m not holding out much hope though as usually at that point in my cycle I’ve always been back in hospital so I figure if I expect it this time I can’t be disappointed!  It’s only a day anyway, in the grand scheme of things it doesn’t really matter…I am upset I’ll have to change my age at the top of this website to 27 though!  I’m really not liking the sound of that!

Well it’s short and sweet today as nothing much as happened and I’m so tired but keep messaging me and I love you all xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
