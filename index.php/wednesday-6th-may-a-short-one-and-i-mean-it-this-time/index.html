Wednesday 6th May – a short one AND I mean it this time!

May 6, 2009 Cara

Evening!

Tonight I am shattered so I won’t write much…I know I always say that but this time it’s true…I hope, or I’ll wake up drooling on my pretty pink laptop having fallen asleep!!  I didn’t sleep too well last night with stomach pains and although I’ve still had a ‘lazy’ day I’ve probably done the most I’ve done in 10 days since this all started so I’m shattered.

The day got off to a bizarre start when lots of parcels arrived (ebay, oops!)  But not all ebay, the wigs I’d ordered to see from the shop who provide 1 free wig via the NHS.  Let’s just say for now that they didn’t look in reality how they looked in the catalogues!!  Me and my mum had a funny morning trying them on though and taking pics!  I’ll attempt to put my wig catalogue so far onto here soon!  So I think I need to make a trip to the store up near the hospital if I’m feeling well enough next time I’m up there.  They provided a great source of entertainment though…especially when Chris got home this evening and tried the blondest of them all on!!  Yes I have a photo!!  But although entertaining it was actually pretty disturbing having them in my house.  It was more fun in the shop.  It seemed like playing in the shop.  Now it seems real and there are these ‘creatures’ hanging around my lounge like rats or ferrets or something!  I even had to order some ‘heads’ online today to put them on when I finally get one or 2 I like (or can put up with!)  Sooooo bizarre, when did ordering wigs and heads become normal?  Shall we look through Next Directory?  Naah let’s look through the wig brochure again!  ODD!  Anyway sure it’ll all work out fine and we’ll all see the funny side…we’re just scared the heads and the wigs will join forces in the night and attack us in our sleep!  I hate dolls and stuff like that, I should never have watched Chucky when I was younger!!  Suffice to say the wigs and the heads will be consigned to the spare room…

…right I’m off, not so much to report today I’m afraid…apart from to say that Anna, Thalia, my mum, Anna’s mum and soon to be Corinne my aunt aswell are now (some running some walking!) Race for Life in aid of Cancer Research UK in Basingstoke on Sunday 21st June.  It’s a great achievement for me to have persuaded my mum and my aunt so please give generously!  I know I’m asking for sponsors non stop but we’ve got some boys v girls rivalry now so I’m sure you’ll all want to help with that!  Or if you’d rather join ‘Team Scamp’ then let me know and you can join them! Go to http://www.raceforlifesponsorme.org/teamscamp.  My other friends Gemma, Lou and Katie are running Race for Life in Reading instead on 19th July so if you’d rather join or sponsor them if it’s more convenient, that’s http://www.raceforlifesponsorme.org/teamcara  It’s a hard life having so many teams!!  Even all my doctors etc. are calling themselves Team Cara!  I don’t know when that became normal either but lots of things that once would’ve been odd are now normal and vice versa!

Right I’m off as I promised myself and you that it’d be short!  You’re probably all still reading last night’s entry anyway!  Keep blogging!!  Or tell me to blog off if you’d prefer!
Love you all

xxx
