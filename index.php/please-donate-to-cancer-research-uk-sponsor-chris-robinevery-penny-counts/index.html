Please donate to Cancer Research UK – sponsor Chris & Robin…every penny counts

April 30, 2009 Cara

Please follow this link to sponsor my brother Robin and my boyfriend Chris.  Quite a few other friends are interested in running it too  on ‘Team Scamp!’ so you can just sponsor them collectively.  Please do though, even if it’s only a small donation, it will help.  I’m so proud of Robin and Chris for doing this for such a good cause in my honour.  I feel really passionately now about raising money for Cancer charities and although I can’t run from my hospital bed…or probably ever again…I can promote those who are doing it for me!!  & I’m sure there’s a million other things we could be doing – let me know your ideas, I’m happy to be promotions manager and chase sponsorships!!

Anyway go to www.justgiving/robinandchris and please donate kindly to something that’s so close to our hearts at the moment.

Much love
