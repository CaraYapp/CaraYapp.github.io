Long time no ‘blogging’!

June 24, 2009 Cara

Hi there!  Well it’s been a long time since I’ve been on here and just to give you some idea of how it’s been…

…fed up…had enough…can’t do it…don’t want to do it…wish had never happened…why did I have to find out?  Why me?  What have I done?  Not fair…so unfair…I’m so unhappy…I can’t be positive…what is there to be positive about?  I can’t stand it…I’m going mad…I’m going crazy…what am I supposed to do?  I wish someone could DO something…I wish I could DO something…I want to punch someone!  No I don’t…I want a punch bag!  I want to throw things around and SCREAM!  I want to curl up in a ball and for it all to disappear…

…just some of the things I’ve been thinking and feeling (& saying!) the last few weeks.  I haven’t been on here as I’ve been either too ill or too upset and fed up…or both!  I think it’s hard for lots of people to understand just how ill I am when I’m really poorly.  Obviously my family and Chris see me through all of it so they know how I am everyday.  I don’t blame people for not understanding…it must be impossible to when you don’t see me.  I’m only really seeing people when I’m a bit better as that’s when I feel up to seeing you but that doesn’t show anyone what I’m like during the really bad times.  I haven’t felt like sharing on here as I feel like I’m all doom and gloom and I don’t want to inflict that on everyone else – that’s no fun to read!  But then those closest to me have pointed out that the point of this blog isn’t actually for your entertainment!  I started it to help me to get everything out of my head and also to help you all to understand how I’m feeling and I’m not helping you to do that if I hide all the negatives.  So I’m sorry if it’s not much fun to read and if I depress you all I apologise but I’m only telling it how it is and hopefully that’ll help me and all of you to get through this…
