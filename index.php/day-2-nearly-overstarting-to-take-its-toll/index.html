Day 2 nearly over…starting to take it’s toll!

April 29, 2009 Cara

I can’t type much as I’m usually speedy but the canula’s in my right wrist now so making it harder.  After the delays last night I’m back on track with chemo but am about 12 or 13 hrs behind schedule cos of it.  I’m on a non chemo drug at the mo which is just to protect my bladder as what I’m having is so powerful it could do some serious damage.  So on this for 18 hours!  So at least I shouldn’t be disturbed too much in the night.  Think it’s starting to take it’d toll as am starting to feel very tired and weary now.  This typing with canula driving me mad so I won’t be long! (remind me of that when I’ve rambled on until you’re about to fall asleep!  I’m good at that…might even send myself to sleep!!)

I’m feeling very lucky to have Chris and my family and friends as they’ve all been truly amazing.  Maybe not so lucky in other respects but I’m still being positive…just worried about when it might run out!!  Thank you so much for everything you’ve all done for me.  If there’s anything you take from this horrid experience, just make sure you look on the bright side of life!  I know it’s a cliche but life really is too short…I know it’s easier said than done but don’t take ANYTHING for granted…believe me I’ve moaned about my hair ALOT in the last 26 years (too long, too short, too this, too that, too dark, too blonde, too GREEN!  BUT let’s not talk about that!) but at least I had hair, now I’m just waiting for it to go, so tempting to shave it, can see why people do to avoid the upset and heartache.  But funnily enough I suddenly love my hair and think I was mental to ever moan!!  You don’t know what you’ve got til it’s gone…or waiting to go!!

Anyway my attitude has totally changed, although I’m apprehensive about trivial things like hair etc. I’m not worried because all I have to focus on is each day as it comes…and for all I care they can chop my leg off(but I hope not!) I just want to beat this thing and come out the other side.  I’ve got so much to live for and that’s all thanks to Chris, my family and friends so thank you!!

So enough of this sentimental rubbish, who else is visiting then?!  My friend Su (there you go Su – you’re officially famous now!) lives in Oxford so this is perfect for us catching up!  She’s popped in yesterday and today.  Gemma (Hewitt not Walton) – there you are, you got your mention…came in today too.  My mum and dad spent the afternoon here, looking at wig catalogues as if you’re just browsing a magazine as if it’s totally normal.  It’s amazing how fast your brain can adapt to what’s going on – I’ve only known 9 days it’s just crazy!  Think Gemma (Walton), Chris O and Dave might be coming tomorrow?  Then Anna and maybe Ollie and Laura on Friday?  Then my bro Robin and maybe Anna again on Saturday, depends when I’ll get released…maybe I’ll be let out early for good behaviour?!  I’ve already got a reputation on the ward for loving pink as my bed is surrounded!  & for talking to everyone!  See – cancer may be nasty but it can’t stop me talking!…or eating….or laughing!  I’m still me!  At least for now…

Anyway I’m off, this canula is going to drive me mad…having a permanant line put in next time so I’ll even have it when I’m at home, but NOOOOOOO before anyone asks, that doesn’t mean you can connect me to a drip full of Magners, or Rose!  Anyway I’d rather taste it!!

Still no pink pee, the stuff I’m on now is clear.  Not sure why I want to tell the world about my pee but hey ho!  I’ve lost all dignity already so no worries about sharing pee stories….in case you wanted to know, EVERY time I go I have to go in a cardboard thing and write my bed number on it so it can be checked!!  Nice!  I don’t think I can cling on to this comparison with me to Carrie from Sex and the City…don’t remember her ever peeing into a cardboard ‘wee bowl’!!  I’ve got the box set at home, plenty of time for me to check!

Right think I’ll get some sleep now, the ‘snorer’ (who will remain anonymous!) in the bed next to me left today so hopefully I’ll sleep better!  She does have a replacement though so we’ll see if she snores too!!  Apparently I talk in my sleep (says Chris!  & my family from when I was younger!) so god knows what I’ll be saying- I’ll give away all my secrets!(not that I have any, I can’t shut up long enough to keep them!!  Well not my own anyway!)

Well just like my talking, my typing goes ooooooooon and ooooooooon and ooooooooon too!  Even when I  said I won’t type much cos of canula.  I really am tired now so think I’ll try to sleep.  I’m supposed to be at a McFly concert now with Laura but obviously I couldn’t go so Ollie’s gone instead.  They keep calling bless them to play songs as I requested but I cannot here a THING just really really loud noise and no songs!!  At least I have it on my ipod though, YAY!

Night night everyone.  I hope some of you manage to do some work whilst reading this in between!

Remember, just be happy with what you’ve got and if you’re not, change it.

I’m so lucky for this to have happened to me at this time when Chris and I couldn’t be more happy and secure and I’ve got my friends and family around me.  Make the most of what you’ve got cos one day you might regret it if you lose some of it temporarily!  I have no regrets as I feel I’ve always done what I’ve wanted to do and followed my heart…if I hadn’t I thnk I’d be going a bit crazy by now with all this time to think you’re bound to start thinking ‘what if…’ but I feel strangely content considering the circumstances.  I just have to let the experts (who have all been amazingly lovely and helpful.  They call themselves Team Cara!!) do what they’ve got to do and try to relax as best I can.  Yes they are poisoning me in effect but it’s to make me better and it WILL make me better.  It’s just hitting home how LONG it’s going to be, the day itself has gone surprisingly quickly but I can’t believe in the grand scheme of things how early on it is and how much worse it’s going to get….but like I said, one day at a time and I WILL get there.

Sleep well everyone and I’ll hope for some more comments tomorrow…I’m loving this!  Remember, anything’s possible – if I can get this evil thing then I can also become an author!  Move over Marian Keyes!  Actually think it’d be more like Bridget Jones meets Jade Goody…but with a HAPPY ENDING!!

Don’t have petty arguments, don’t sleep on an argument and make the best of what you’ve got.  We are all lucky in ways and unlucky in others.  If I had no support or Chris or friends or family then I’d be a hell of a lot worse than this so just appreciate who you’ve got.  I know you all do anyway but it’s so easy to take things and people for granted and to obsess or stress or worry about trivial things.  I know you won’t all change your thinking overnight but I feel like I have.  I know the people who care about me will love me no matter what, hair or no hair, fat or thin (I’m hoping to get down to 7 stone on chemo!  I hear it can be done!)  Although at the mo I’m eating like a horse to stock up in case I get sick soon!  The food here’s really nice!  Think I’m the annoying new kid on the block as I’m stuffing my face and loving it, everyone else is moaning.  Just proves I’m easily pleased!!  Some of them are alot further on with treatment so are off their food, so I’ve got that to look forward to?!  But apparently I’m having more chemo and for longer than them.  Lots of them haven’t lost their hair, but with my chemo combination of drugs, I will.

Anyway the point I was trying to make was that so many things we stress about really really really just do not MATTER!  Just ask yourself  ‘does it matter?!’  The answer’s probably no!  Obviously putting things into perspective also has to be realistic, we can’t ignore all the trivial things or we wouldn’t be human!  I’ve just forgotten so quickly what it’s like to worry about the day to day things like planning a lesson for school or getting all my marking done or the housework or what I look like etc. etc.  My attitude now is if I’m alive it’s good enough… I know it sounds stupid but it’s true!  Having no hair, having a knee replacement, all these things that seemed like the end of the world, really don’t anymore.  Because the end of the world will not happen as long as I’m still here and I just know I’ll grow to be an oooooooold laaaaaaaaadyyyyyyy!!

Oh dear, this is starting to sound like a self-help book now and that’s really NOT what I was aiming for!…

‘How to bore your friends and family to insanity in 1 easy step: go all sentimental on them and start spurting out stereotypical advice which will make them want to puke!’

By Cara Yapp

If any publishers or magazine editors are reading this, I promise if you sign me up I won’t go down that path again…much!

Just a simple bye will do now cos I can’t shut up!  I might post on here what I wrote at home the first night I found out about this…if I’m feeling brave.  I haven’t read it since that night and god knows what it said!  Feels like I’ve been drunk or dreaming for the last 9 days and I’ll soon wake up with a hangover…no such luck!!  & I haven’t been drinking…honestly!!

Love and hugs

Scamp xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
